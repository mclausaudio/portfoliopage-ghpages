    About

    Hello, my name is Michael. I'm a professional sound designer and self-taught software developer from San Francisco, California. I'm the founder of an online radio platform called SutroFM and a record label called Silva Electronics. I'm also a published musician, with multiple releases in a variety of formats, ranging from full length albums on cassette to 4-track EPs on 12" vinyl record. I began teaching myself JavaScript in 2014 and continue to use it as my primary programing language. You can check out some of my projects below.
