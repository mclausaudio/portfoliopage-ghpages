    About

    Hello, my name is Michael. I'm a Full Stack Developer from San Francisco, California. I'm the founder of an online radio station called SutroFM.net and a record label called Silva Electronics. I began teaching myself JavaScript in 2014 and continue to use it as my primary programing language. You can view examples of my work below. I'm also a published musician, with releases in a variety of formats ranging from 12" vinyl to cassette to digital. Programming, technology and music are passions of mine and I am constantly seeking out new and creative ways to fuse them to foster positive social change and self-expression.
